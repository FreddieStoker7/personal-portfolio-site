A bit about me

I've always felt a little lost, and have never been quite sure where my life was going. I've tried all sorts of jobs in my short working life, went to uni, dropped out, nothing seemed to quite ever click. But shortly before christmas 2021 I wrote my first few lines of javascript and you could say that a lightbulb went off in my brain. This was the perfect blend of logic, problem solving and creativity that my brain had been craving and I needed more of it. On a whim I quit an apprenticeship with promising prospects and went all in on software development, you can't change your life without taking risks after all. Less than a week on from first finding out what Javascript was, I was taking my entry challenge for Northcoders. I managed to sail through and gained a place in the January cohort via an excellence scholarship.

Northcoder's turned out to be one of the most taxing yet fulfilling learning experiences of my life. I crammed more information than I thought possible into my head in the space of 12 short weeks. For the first time in my life 8 hour working days seem to breeze by, you might say i'd found some sort of flow state and I was genuinley loving the experience of learning how to code. I think the thing that excites me so much about this field is that it is constantly evolving, and the learning never stops which is really what I needed and had lacked in my previous roles.

Aside from my new found passion for coding, I have have always had a love of all sport. Mainly football, golf and cricket growing up but these days I like going off on adventures with my mountain bike and a tent, or skiing down black runs in the alps. I have also recently been on a few surfing trips and am more than a bit envious of anyone who lives by the sea!
